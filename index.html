ASET Core Overview

ASET (AeroSystems Engineering Tool) Core is a modular, mission-level simulation engine designed to couple propulsion, aerodynamics, controls, and vehicle dynamics within a single architecture. It provides a clear, extensible framework for modeling aerospace systems with research-grade numerical methods. The engine is built for teams that require accuracy, modularity, and integrated aerospace vehicle analysis.

5-Year Roadmap

  • 2026: Release prototype demo with core airplane subsystem coupling and mission-level simulation.
  • 2027: Expand subsystem library (structures, guidance, thermal) and begin external pilot programs.
  • 2028: Launch v1.0 with full modular architecture and documentation; initiate scramjet R&D integration.
  • 2029: Collaborate with AFRL, NASA, and NRL; support advanced hypersonic mission modeling.
  • 2030: Establish ASET Core as a trusted simulation platform across aerospace R&D and defense programs.
